Thespian Honor Society

The Oakton High School Theatre Arts Department engages, inspires, entertains and challenges students and audiences with theatrical productions that range from the classics to new works. Through our programs (Theatre Arts 1-4, Technical Theatre 1-4), we provide arts education that promote life-long skills. We encourage exposure to travel and experiences that go beyond our own stage and we celebrate the essential power of the theatre to illuminate our common humanity. Shows are directed by our beloved Theatre Arts Director, Mrs. Vanessa Gelinas.
